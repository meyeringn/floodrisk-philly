Plain-language summary FloodRisk Philly does not require an account and does not maintain a database of searched addresses. What leaves your browser A search uses outside services needed to complete the lookup. • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: receives the cleaned street address to locate it. • FEMA National Flood Hazard Layer: receives latitude and longitude coordinates to check the mapped flood zone. • OpenStreetMap map tiles: receives map-tile requests needed to display the map. What stays in your browser Apartment and accessibility answers are used to generate personalized guidance in the browser. FloodRisk Philly does not send those answers to FEMA or OpenStreetMap. • Floor category • Elevator or stair needs • Powered equipment needs • Evacuation-assistance needs • Pet planning • Exit constraints Storage The site may use local browser storage to remember the selected language. It does not intentionally store searched addresses, FEMA results, renter-profile answers, or checklist selections. A temporary in-memory cache may prevent repeated address requests during one open page session. It clears when the page is closed or refreshed. Analytics and tracking The rebuilt version does not include advertising trackers, behavioral analytics, user accounts, or fingerprinting code. Safer use Enter only the street address needed for the lookup. Do not enter names, medical diagnoses, passwords, account numbers, or detailed personal notes. External-service logs OpenStreetMap and FEMA may receive normal technical request information, such as an IP address, browser information, and request time. Their own policies govern that processing.
