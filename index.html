About FloodRisk Philly

This tool is built for renters — people who don't own their homes but still bear the real costs of flooding: damaged belongings, displacement, missed work, and inaccessible exits.

Data sources: FEMA National Flood Hazard Layer, OpenDataPhilly stormwater and impervious surface data, Philadelphia Water Department.

Built as part of Vibe Coding for Climate Action — open-source civic tech for Philadelphia. View source on GitHub ↗
